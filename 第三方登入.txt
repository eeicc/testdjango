參考網址:https://django-allauth.readthedocs.io/en/latest/

1.  pip install django-allauth 先安裝套件


2.  新增以下設定到settings.py
    'django.contrib.sites',
    'allauth',
    'allauth.account',
    'allauth.socialaccount',
    # 把需要的第三方登入都放這裡
    'allauth.socialaccount.providers.github', 

3.  額外新增 "AUTHENTICATION_BACKENDS" 到settings.py
    AUTHENTICATION_BACKENDS = (
    # Needed to login by username in Django admin, regardless of `allauth`
    'django.contrib.auth.backends.ModelBackend',
    # `allauth` specific authentication methods, such as login by e-mail
    'allauth.account.auth_backends.AuthenticationBackend',
    )
    SITE_ID = 1

4.  INSTALLED_APPS有更改，要記得下python manage.py migrate

5.  調整URL: path('accounts/', include('allauth.urls')),

6.  創建第三方平台OAuth

7.  到網站設定更改網域，進到社群應用程式新增OAuth的序號(ID & Key)
